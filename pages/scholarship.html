Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for scholarships?

Eligibility criteria vary for each scholarship, but most focus on academic performance and financial need.

How can I apply for scholarships?

Visit the official scholarship website and fill out the application form with required documents.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships at the same time?

Yes, as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each scholarship.

What if I miss the deadline?

You cannot apply after the deadline, but you can prepare for the next cycle if the scholarship is recurring.

Will I get the scholarship every year automatically?

Some scholarships are renewable every year based on your academic performance, while others are one-time only.
