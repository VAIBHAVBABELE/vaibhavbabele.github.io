Privacy Policy

Welcome to Nitra Mitra. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

We collect the following types of information:
• Personal identification (name, email, student ID)

• Device and browser information
• Usage patterns and preferences
• Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:
• Personalize your learning experience
• Improve our educational services
• Send important notifications
• Maintain platform security
• Analyze usage patterns

3. Data Security

We implement various security measures including:
• Encryption of sensitive data
• Regular security assessments
• Access controls
• Secure data storage
• Regular backups

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:
• Access your personal data
• Request data correction
• Delete your account
• Opt-out of communications
• Export your data

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for:
• Educational purposes
• Legal requirements
• Account maintenance
• Service improvement

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us:
• Email: nitramitra@gmail.com
• Visit our Contact Page
• Response time: Within 48 hours
